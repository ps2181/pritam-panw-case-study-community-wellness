    AI Filtering Criteria

    The AI classifies a submission as noise if it matches any of these patterns:

    • Personal frustration or venting without a specific safety incident
    • Requests for services (plumbers, recommendations, etc.)
    • Duplicate reports of the same verified incident
    • Unverifiable rumors without corroborating detail
    • General opinion or political commentary

    ⚠️ Fallback: If the AI is unavailable, a rule-based keyword classifier is used. Moderators review all borderline cases.
